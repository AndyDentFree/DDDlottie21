Lottie

Simple animated vectors from Adobe After Effects via bodymovin plugin to JSON, effectively a standard invented in 2015 with web player.

Fast native players by AirBnb 2017 onwards

Visual editors we will be talking about:
Keyshape, Flow and Synfig.

Why does Text Matter?

Lottie is text just like SVG
(except JSON rather than <XML>)

Easily parsed, hand-edited,
& diffed by version control.
